Specialized clinics

Antenatal Clinic

Care and support for expectant mothers before delivery.

Our antenatal clinic offers routine checkups, ultrasounds, and expert advice to ensure the health of both the mother and the baby during pregnancy.

Pre-Natal & Post-Natal Clinics

Comprehensive care before and after childbirth.

Our pre-natal and post-natal clinics provide expert guidance and support during pregnancy and after delivery, ensuring a smooth recovery and well-being.

Family Planning

Expert advice on family planning methods and reproductive health.

Our family planning clinic offers a variety of methods, including contraceptives, counseling, and education on reproductive health.

Gynecology

Care for female reproductive health.

Our gynecology clinic provides routine examinations, treatment for reproductive health issues, and specialized care for women at every stage of life.

Ear, Throat & Nose

Expert care for ear, throat, and nose conditions.

Our ENT clinic offers treatment for hearing problems, ear infections, throat conditions, and sinus issues with advanced diagnostic tools and therapies.

Colonoscopy

Screening for colon health and early detection of diseases.

Our colonoscopy service offers early detection of colorectal diseases and conditions through a simple and safe procedure.

Endoscopy

Advanced diagnostic procedure for the digestive system.

Endoscopy allows our specialists to visually examine the digestive tract, identify problems, and conduct necessary treatments with minimal discomfort.

Physician

General medical consultations and healthcare services.

Our physicians offer consultations for a wide range of medical concerns, diagnosing, treating, and managing both acute and chronic conditions.

Psychologist

Therapeutic support for mental health and emotional well-being.

Orthopedic

Specialized care for bone, joint, and muscle conditions.

Our orthopedic clinic offers diagnosis and treatment for bone and joint conditions, including arthritis, fractures, and sports injuries.

Occupational Therapy

Helping patients regain their ability to perform everyday tasks.

Dermatologist

Surgeon Review
